A Life of Tender Mercies
In Memoriam

Dee Merlin Stevens

November 3, 1950  —  May 22, 2026
✦

Dee Merlin Stevens, age 75, passed away in his sleep in the early hours of May 22nd, 2026.

His final months—despite being fraught with illness, injury, and declining health—were focused on family history and temple work. He spent hours in the temple every chance he got until his body would not allow it.

His other passion was a collection of memoirs now published online as "A Life of Tender Mercies." Every visitor over the past few months would get at least one chapter read to them, whether they wanted it or not.

He longed for the day he would be reunited with his sweetheart, Kathy, and spoke of it often. We trust it has been a beautiful reunion with her, and with the many other dear family members who preceded him.

The funeral is being arranged to take place on June 5th. A full obituary and funeral details will be published here soon.

"She is not gone. She is ahead. I intend to catch up."

He caught up.
May 22, 2026.
